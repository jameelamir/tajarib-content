# YouTube Description Format — تجارب

## Version
v1.0 — 2026-02-21

## The Format

```
[Paragraph 1: What this episode is about — the premise, the guest's world, why this conversation matters now. Conversational, flowing prose. 3-5 lines. Iraqi white language.]

[Paragraph 2: What listeners will hear — key ideas, tensions, or revelations covered in the episode. Still prose, not bullets. 3-5 lines.]

---
🎙️ [Guest Name] — [Title / Role / Organization]

📌 أقسام الحلقة:
00:00 المقدمة
[chapters...]

---
[Sponsor mention — 1-2 lines, natural and contextual, not promotional. Near bottom but not last.]

---
تابع تجارب على جميع المنصات:
🔗 www.tajarib.show
```

## Rules
- Prose, not bullet points — unless there's a strong reason otherwise.
- Guest opinions always framed as their perspective, not stated as fact.
- Sponsor mention: natural, near bottom, not the final thing the reader sees.
- No exaggeration or marketing fluff. Smart, calm, credible.
- Language: Arabic (Iraqi white language) — unless instructed otherwise.
- Don't use چ → use ج. Say وية not ويا.
